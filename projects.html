    Improving COVID-19 forecasts: A comparison with CDC models Permalink

    Improving COVID-19 forecasts: A comparison with CDC models is a research project that aims to enhance the accuracy of COVID-19 forecasting models. This project involves a detailed analysis and comparison of COVID-19 forecasts with the models provided by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

    COVID-19 knowledge graphs Permalink

    COVID-19 knowledge graphs are data structures that organize pandemic-related information in a connected graph format, enabling researchers and healthcare professionals to easily explore and analyze diverse data sources for insights and solutions in the fight against COVID-19. They provide a comprehensive, interconnected view of information, improving data integration and decision-making processes.
